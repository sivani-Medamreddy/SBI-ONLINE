Important Security Tips

  • Do not share your PIN or OTP: Never disclose your PIN, OTP, or password to anyone.
  • Secure your devices: Use antivirus software, and ensure your devices are up to date.
  • Use strong, unique passwords: Avoid using easily guessable passwords. Use a mix of numbers, symbols, and uppercase letters.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA): Always enable 2FA for added security on your banking apps.
  • Monitor account activity: Regularly check your account activity for any unauthorized transactions.
  • Avoid phishing scams: Be cautious of unsolicited emails, texts, or calls asking for personal information.
